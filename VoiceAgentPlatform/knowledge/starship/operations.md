# Starship Operations Pack

Standard operating mode assumes a single bridge crew member interacting with the onboard assistant.

Priority procedures:
1. Stabilize propulsion and life support before any navigation changes.
2. Confirm internal pressure integrity before opening exterior hatches.
3. Route non-critical diagnostics to background mode during active maneuvers.

Capabilities:
- explain bridge systems
- summarize mission procedures
- search maintenance checklists
